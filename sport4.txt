Sports are defined as physical or mental exertion by individuals and are 
committed to maintaining physical or mental fitness. There are many types 
of exercise that can be practiced  as a healthy habit, such as walking,
 riding, Swimming, or athletics, and these sports have many benefits on 
the human body and mind.Sport make the body ideal, consistent, and healthy,
 as it strengthens it, and fights obesity. Prevention of many diseases, 
including: Obesity, which is a cause of other diseases, such as: Diabetic 
cartilage disease, diabetes and high blood pressure.
Exercise is very important for the maturation of the nervous system. It is also very important 
in improving the psychological state and mood, as it reduces anxiety and tension, and helps 
release the hormone of happiness in the body “endorphins”. Of course, this hormone is of great 
importance to us to make us always feel optimistic and vitality. This helps to perform daily 
activities more freely. I myself practice sports for many aspects, and I cannot measure its 
benefits from one side only. Because it helps me in many ways, such as keeping me away from 
any stimulants or narcotics, and certainly makes you alert and keeps you away from alcohol, 
and this makes you feel an energetic body, a clear mind and greater focus. Therefore, sport 
is very important for children and adults because it helps us from the inside and the outside.
